Beans on Toast

Description

The perfect breakfast to kickstart your day prepared in less than 10 minutes

Ingridients

  • 1/2 Can of beans
  • A slice of bread
  • As much or as little butter as you'd like

Steps

  1. Pour 1/2 can of beans into a small pot or a saucepan
  2. While the beans are in the pot, put a slice of bread into your toaster
  3. Once the the bread is finished toasting, just add a spread of butter and pour your pot of beans over the buttered toast.
  4. Enjoy !
