Egg omlette

Description

Lovely breakfast full of protein to get you ready

Ingridients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Break all your eggs in a bowl and whisk until very very smooth.
  2. Add a bit of butter to a frying pan
  3. Pour the smooth egg mixture into a frying pan and move arround with a spatula until the the base thickens
  4. Flip one half of the omlette over
  5. Enjoy !
