Tuna and sweetcorn sandwich

Description

A quick to make delicious sandwich for on the go

Ingridients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 1 Can of tuna
  • 1 Can of sweetcorn
  • A bit of mayo if you fancy

Steps

  1. Mix tuna sweetcorn and mayo (if you wish) inside a small bowl or container
  2. Spread your mix on the bread
  3. Slice diagonally for a better look
  4. Enjoy !
